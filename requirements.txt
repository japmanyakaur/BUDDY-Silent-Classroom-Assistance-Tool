  Vega board – the main microcontroller 

  Touch sensor – to detect taps or long presses

  RGB LED  – to show different light signals

  Servo motor (SG90) – to raise and rotate a small flag or marker

  Jumper wires – to connect everything

  USB cable  – to power the Vega board

  Small flag (paper and stick) – attach to the servo to show task is done

  Breadboard  – to make temporary connections

  Cardboard  base – to mount everything neatly on the desk
